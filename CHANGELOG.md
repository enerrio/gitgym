## [0.1.0] - 2026-02-21

### Added

- Initial release of gitgym
- 32 exercises across 9 topics (basics, committing, branching, merging, history, undoing, rebase, stashing, advanced)
- CLI commands: `list`, `start`, `next`, `describe`, `verify`, `watch`, `hint`, `reset`, `progress`, `clean`
- Progressive hint system
- Watch mode with automatic re-verification
- Progress tracking saved to `~/.gitgym/progress.json`
- macOS and Linux support
